About Me

Hi, I’m Wei Yang. I am Graphic Design student from TARUMT. I start photography in 2023 because school required, but I am also interested in photography. Although I am interested in photography, I still have a lot to learn and improve in photography.

I’d like to try something different when I get the chance. For example, portrait photography and sports photography. I have seen a lot of good-looking portrait photography works, and I have tried to shoot portraits, but I feel that my current equipment is not enough for me to take good portraits. If I add more equipment, I will try to shoot more portraits photography and sports photography.
